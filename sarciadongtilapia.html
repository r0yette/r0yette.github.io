Sarciadong Tilapia

This is a recipe for Sarciadong Tilapia

Prep: 10 minutes

Cooking: 30 minutes

Total: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • Tilapia (Fish) (cleaned)
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1-2 Onion (diced)
  • 2-4 Tomatoes (diced)
  • 3/4 - 1 cup water
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1 tbsp Fish Sauce (Patis)
  • Cooking Oil

How to Cook Sarciadong Tilapia

  • Pan Fry the Tilapia. Set aside.
  • In a separate pan, saute garlic, onion and tomatoes.
  • Add Water and simmer for 5 minutes
  • Add the beaten egg and fish sauce. Cook for 3 minutes.
  • Add the Tilapia and cook for 5 minutes.
  • Serve hot.
  • Enjoy :)
